## Hall of Fame Criteria

To be inducted into the Hall of Fame, contributors must meet one or more of the following:

### Impact Criteria

- **Code**: 50+ high-quality PRs merged OR major architectural contribution
- **Security**: Discovery and responsible disclosure of a critical vulnerability
- **Documentation**: Comprehensive documentation improvement project
- **Community**: 6+ months of consistent, exceptional community support
- **Innovation**: Introduction of a feature that significantly improves user experience

### Values Criteria

All Hall of Fame inductees must also demonstrate:

- ‚úÖ Adherence to our [Code of Conduct](../../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- ‚úÖ Constructive and helpful communication
- ‚úÖ Willingness to mentor and help others
- ‚úÖ Consistent quality in contributions

## Nomination Process

### How to Nominate

1. Open a [discussion](https://github.com/rinafcode/teachLink_contract/discussions/new?category=hall-of-fame-nominations) with:
   - Nominee's GitHub username
   - Category for recognition
   - Detailed description of their contributions
   - Links to relevant PRs, issues, or community interactions

2. Nominations are reviewed quarterly by maintainers

3. Inductees are announced in project releases and community channels

### Self-Nomination

Self-nominations are accepted! If you believe you've made significant contributions that haven't been recognized, please nominate yourself with supporting evidence.
