# Contributors

Thank you to everyone who has contributed to TeachLink! 🎓

Contributions of all kinds count: code, documentation, bug reports, community support, mentoring, and more. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for how to get involved.

---

## Contributor Tiers

| Tier | Requirements |
|------|--------------|
| 🌱 Newcomer | First contribution merged |
| 🌿 Contributor | 3+ contributions merged |
| 🌳 Regular Contributor | 10+ contributions merged |
| 🏆 Core Contributor | 25+ contributions + consistent quality |
| ⭐ Maintainer | Invited by existing maintainers |

To request a tier upgrade, open a PR updating your entry below. See [CONTRIBUTING.md § How to Claim Recognition](CONTRIBUTING.md#how-to-claim-recognition).

## How to Add Yourself

When your first PR is merged, open a follow-up PR that:

1. Adds your entry to the **Newcomers** section (or the appropriate tier).
2. Uses this format:

```markdown
| [@yourusername](https://github.com/yourusername) | Brief description of contribution |
```

Maintainers may also add contributors directly after merging.
